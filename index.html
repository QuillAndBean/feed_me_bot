Feed Me Bot is a service that will suggest meals from curated by you. Feed Me Bot will only suggest your favorite foods and make ordering a cinch.

Too Much of a Good Thing

Are you a busy professional with more meetings than you can list? Are you too busy to browse through page after page of menus? A single SMS to Feed Me Bot can save you a world of stress. All you have to do is text the Feed Me Bot that you're hungry and it will suggest a meal from your curated favorites list.

Calling to order your food is so 1999. Ordering your food on a web page is so 2009. Want to know what's so 2019? Ordering your food with Feed Me Bot is. Like a suggestion? Just text "Yes" to the bot and you can get back to your day.

Curated Suggestions
